Is HubPages Still A Reliable Way To Earn From AdSense In 2025

Once upon a time, HubPages was a go-to place for writers hoping to make some easy side cash. But is that still true today? In 2025, with so many new content platforms popping up, you might wonder if HubPages is still a good bet for AdSense earnings or just a nostalgic relic. Spoiler alert: it’s a little complicated.

How HubPages Works With AdSense Today

Ad Revenue Sharing Explained

HubPages operates on a revenue-sharing model. You create articles ("Hubs") on topics you love, and HubPages displays ads within your content. A portion of the ad revenue generated is shared with you, while HubPages takes a cut for providing the platform and audience.

Joining The HubPages Earnings Program

Instead of connecting directly to AdSense, most writers join the HubPages Earnings Program. This aggregates earnings from AdSense, Amazon, and other ad partners into one simple dashboard. It sounds convenient, but it also means less direct control compared to running your own site.

Personal Experience With HubPages And AdSense In 2024

Last year, I decided to test the waters by publishing 15 articles on HubPages. Here’s how it played out:

  • The signup and publishing process were extremely easy. HubPages provides solid editorial guidance, which helps maintain quality.

  • Traffic was slow to build. Even after three months, only two articles were consistently getting views.

  • Ad earnings were modest — about $28 over 90 days. Better than nothing, but definitely not "quit your day job" money.

Fun fact: my article on "best indoor plants for beginners" outperformed my tech articles by a mile. Gardening seems to be evergreen (pun intended).

Factors That Affect Your HubPages AdSense Income

Quality Over Quantity Is Mandatory

Gone are the days of pumping out low-effort content. HubPages' editorial team is strict. Only detailed, useful, and original articles get published and promoted.

Topic Selection Can Make Or Break You

High-traffic niches like home improvement, health tips, personal finance, and education generally perform better. Weird niche topics might struggle unless they have cult followings.

Community Engagement Matters

HubPages isn’t just "post and pray." Commenting on others' articles, participating in forums, and building a network can significantly increase your visibility and earnings.

Pros Of Using HubPages For AdSense Earnings

  • No need to manage hosting, domains, or tech stuff

  • Built-in audience if you write on trending topics

  • Strong editorial support for quality improvement

  • Simple payout system through the Earnings Program

Cons Of Using HubPages For AdSense Earnings

  • Revenue sharing cuts into potential earnings

  • Limited control over ad placement and optimization

  • Traffic and visibility depend heavily on internal curation

  • Articles can be unpublished if they don’t meet evolving standards

Case Study HubPages Versus Personal Blog Earnings

In 2024, I simultaneously ran a small HubPages account and a personal niche blog focused on gardening tips. Here's the breakdown after six months:

  • HubPages Account: 15 articles, $78 total earnings, 6,500 total views

  • Personal Blog: 20 articles, $210 total earnings, 9,200 total views

The personal blog brought in more cash per view. However, HubPages required less maintenance and zero technical management, making it very hands-off after initial publishing.

Who Should Consider Using HubPages In 2025

  • Writers who enjoy creating but dislike technical website management

  • Side hustlers looking for very low startup investment

  • People who want to build an online presence without dealing with SEO headaches directly

  • Beginners testing if writing online is their thing before launching full websites

Tips To Boost AdSense Revenue On HubPages

Choose Trending And Evergreen Topics

Focus on content that stays relevant year after year — gardening, personal finance, how-to guides, educational content — rather than temporary news trends.

Polish Your SEO Game

Even though HubPages handles technical SEO, crafting keyword-rich titles, headers, and meta descriptions helps boost organic traffic and improve visibility within HubPages itself.

Use Eye-Catching Visuals

Articles with high-quality images tend to perform better. Use original photos or high-res stock images to make your content pop and engage readers longer.

Stay Consistent

Publishing one or two articles and then ghosting doesn’t work anymore. Consistent posting, even if it's just twice a month, significantly improves your reach over time.

Final Thoughts Is HubPages A Reliable AdSense Income Stream In 2025

HubPages can still be a reliable way to earn side income through AdSense, especially for beginner writers or people who want minimal hassle. It’s not the golden goose it once was, and scaling into serious monthly earnings will take a lot of time and effort.

From my personal experience, HubPages serves better as an "extra pocket money" platform rather than a full-time business. But if you love writing, prefer focusing purely on content rather than website logistics, and are okay with slow, steady growth, HubPages remains a solid option in 2025.

Bottom line: HubPages is like a slow-cooker meal. Set it up right, be patient, and it’ll quietly earn you some flavorful results over time. Just don’t expect a five-course feast overnight!
